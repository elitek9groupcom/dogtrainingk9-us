FAQ

Direct answers for owners comparing dog training options in California.

How do I choose between private sessions and dog boarding and training programs?

If speed and consistency are the priority, board-and-train can accelerate baseline control. Private sessions are ideal when you want weekly coaching integrated into your routine. Elite K9 Group can advise based on your dog and timeline.

What is included in service dog training cost planning?

Service pathways factor in evaluation, obedience reliability, task foundation, handler transfer, and public access rehearsal. Costs depend on scope, not one-size-fits-all bundles.

Do you cover German Shepherd puppies for sale in Los Angeles, CA?

This site does not list puppies directly. For available dogs, planned breedings, and trained working prospects, use the official Elite K9 Group pages linked here.

Can a dog with serious fear or reactivity still improve?

Yes, with correct thresholds, consistent structure, and safe progression. Confidence building for dogs is a core part of quality training for aggressive dogs and reactivity cases.
